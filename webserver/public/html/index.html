I've been part of a martial arts club called Ninjutsu for the past 3 years. Over the 3 years I've been in it, less and less people have joined each semester. There's a good chance this upcoming fall semester will be the last semester for the club, as we do not have enough people to continue. If you're interested in coming to a meeting and trying it out, the first two classes on Tuesday September 10th and Thursday September 12th at 7:30pm are free and open to everyone. We train in the Combatives Room in the Athletics Center. This room can be found by going up the stairs to the second level, going through the lounge and turning left at the bathrooms, and taking another left through a door. Any questions can be emailed to ninjutsu@uoguelph.ca.

I would really appreciate it if some of you came to meet us and check out the club. It's a really friendly and fun group of people.
